4 Reasons College Students Love CengageNOW

Take a Look

  • Elevate Thinking

    CengageNOW Enhances Student Engagement

    All instructors reported that students’ engagement was improved by their use of CengageNOW, with more than a third agreeing that engagement was greatly impacted. Ninety-seven percent of students agreed that they found the coursework to be very (62%) or somewhat (35%) engaging.

  • Promote Better Outcomes

    Grades Increased Dramatically Using CengageNOW

    Students using CengageNOW achieved significantly higher final end-of-course grades in accounting than did students in classes using the same textbooks but without CengageNOW. This level of growth reflects an increase from the 50th to the 64th percentile (14 percentile points).

  • Easily Set Your Course

    CengageNOW Improves Outcomes, Makes Teaching Easier

    All 15 instructors using CengageNOW felt that it contributed to the improvement of student learning outcomes, with 93 percent calling the outcomes greatly (33%) or somewhat (60%) improved. Instructors also agreed that CengageNOW made teaching easier and better (average rating of 3.6 out of 4).

  • CengageNOW Meets the Needs of All Types of Students

    Instructors agreed that CengageNOW is equally effective for men and women and for students of different ethnicities. In addition, instructors stated the needs of students with a wide range of capabilities were met very well through the use of CengageNOW (average rating of 3.3 on a scale of 1-4). And 81% of the 27 students reported learning more than they would have otherwise using CengageNOW.
